Deep Learning

Author: Shubham Ramdas Lokhande

Date: 28 Jan 2020

Deep learning is a subfield of machine learning that focuses on the development and training of artificial neural networks to perform tasks without explicit programming. The term "deep" refers to the use of deep neural networks, which consist of multiple layers of interconnected nodes (artificial neurons) to model complex patterns and representations in data. Unlike traditional machine learning models, deep learning excels at automatically learning hierarchical features from raw data, making it particularly effective in tasks such as image and speech recognition, natural language processing, and complex decision-making.
